\begin{mybox}[Definition of Trigonometric Functions] Let \(t\) be any real number and let \(P(x,y)\) be the terminal point on the unit circle associated with \(t\text{.}\) Then

\begin{align*} \sin t\amp =y \amp \cos t\amp =x \amp \tan t\amp =\frac{y}{x} (x\neq0)\\ \csc t\amp =\frac{1}{y} (y\neq0) \amp \sec t\amp =\frac{1}{x} (x\neq0) \amp \cot t\amp =\frac{x}{y} (y\neq0) \end{align*}
