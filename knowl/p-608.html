The distance between \(A\) and \(B\) is

\begin{align*} d(A,B)\amp =\amp \sqrt{(\cos(\alpha-\beta)-1)^2+(\sin(\alpha-\beta)-0)^2}\\ \amp =\amp \sqrt{\cos^2(\alpha-\beta)-2\cos(\alpha-\beta)+1+\sin^2(\alpha-\beta)}\\ \amp =\amp \sqrt{(\cos^2(\alpha-\beta)+\sin^2(\alpha-\beta))-2\cos(\alpha-\beta)+1}\\ \amp =\amp \sqrt{1-2\cos(\alpha-\beta)+1}\\ \amp =\amp \sqrt{2-2\cos(\alpha-\beta)} \end{align*}
