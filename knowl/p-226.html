Answers produced by calculators are estimates and you should pay close attention to see if the question is asking for the exact solution or a decimal approximation. For example, if you need to calculate \(\sin45^{\circ}=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\text{,}\) the calculator may give the answer as \(\sin45^{\circ}\approx0.70710678\text{,}\) which is a decimal approximation since the actual solution goes on forever. Unless stated otherwise, answers in the book should be exact, e.g. \(\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\) and not 0.70710678.
