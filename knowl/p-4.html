The four cardinal points correspond to a house. The stars arrive from the horizon called Hikina ( “To Arrive” ) or East and enter the horizon called Komohana ( “To Enter” ) or West. If you stand with your back towards Hikina (East) and face Komohana (West), your right will point towards ʻĀkau ( “Right” ) or North and your left will point towards Hema ( “Left” ) or South.
