Item 2.

Since \(-770^{\circ}\lt 0\text{,}\) we first add \(3\times360\) to \(-770^{\circ}\) to obtain an angle \(0^{\circ}\) and \(360^{\circ}\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} -770^{\circ}+3\times360^{\circ}=310^{\circ} \end{equation*}

So \(310^{\circ}\) and \(-770^{\circ}\) are coterminal. Since \(270^{\circ}\lt 310^{\circ}\lt 360^{\circ}\text{,}\) \(310^{\circ}\) lies in Quadrant IV, or Manu Malanai.
