Using Heron's Formula, we begin by calculating the semiperimeter

\begin{equation*} s=\frac{1}{2}(4,250+4,500+4,450)=6,600 \end{equation*}

Then the area created by the Polynesian Triangle is

\begin{equation*} \mbox{Area} =\sqrt{6,600(6,600-4,250)(6,600-4,500)(6,600-4,450)}\approx8,368,253\mbox{mi} ^2 \end{equation*}
