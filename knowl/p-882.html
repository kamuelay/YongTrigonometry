We can verify that this is in fact a unit vector by computing the magnitude:

\begin{equation*} \|\mathbf{u}\|=\left\|\left\langle\frac{2\sqrt{5}}{5},-\frac{\sqrt{5}}{5}\right\rangle\right\|=\sqrt{\left(\frac{2\sqrt{5}}{5}\right)^2+\left(-\frac{\sqrt{5}}{5}\right)^2}=\sqrt{\frac{4\cdot5}{25}+\frac{5}{25}}=\sqrt{\frac{25}{25}}=1 \end{equation*}
