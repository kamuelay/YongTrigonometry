First we will convert \(0.625^{\circ}\) to minutes using the conversion \(1^{\circ}=60'\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} 0.625^{\circ}=0.625^{\circ}\cdot\frac{60'}{1^{\circ}}=37.5' \end{equation*}

Since \(1'=60''\text{,}\) we can convert \(0.5'\) to seconds: \(\displaystyle 0.5'=0.5'\cdot\frac{60''}{1'}=30''\text{.}\)

So \(140.625^{\circ}=140^{\circ}37'30''\text{.}\)
