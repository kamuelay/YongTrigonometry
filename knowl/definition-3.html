Definition 1.2.6.

If an angle is drawn on the \(xy\)-plane and the vertex is at the origin and the initial side is on the positive \(x\)-axis, then that angle is said to be in standard position. If the angle is measured in a counterclockwise rotation, the angle is said to be a positive angle and if the angle is measured in a clockwise rotation, the angle is said to be a negative angle.
