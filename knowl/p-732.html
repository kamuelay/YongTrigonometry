To solve an oblique triangle, we must know the length of one side as well as two other values of the triangle. This gives us a total of 5 possible triangles.

  1. Angle-Side-Angle (ASA): Two angles and the side between them are known

  2. Side-Angle-Angle (SAA): One side and two consecutive angles are known

  3. Side-Side-Angle (SSA): Two sides and the angle that is not between them are known

  4. Side-Angle-Side (SAS): Two sides and the angle between them are known

  5. Side-Side-Side (SSS): All three sides of a triangle are known
