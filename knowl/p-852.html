If \(\mathbf{u}=\langle u_x,u_y\rangle\text{,}\) \(\mathbf{v}=\langle v_x,v_y\rangle\text{,}\) and \(c\) is a scalar, then

\begin{align*} \mathbf{u}+\mathbf{v}\amp =\amp \langle u_x+v_x,u_y+v_y\rangle\\ \mathbf{u}-\mathbf{v}\amp =\amp \langle u_x-v_x,u_y-v_y\rangle\\ c\mathbf{u}\amp =\amp \langle cu_x,cu_y\rangle \end{align*}
