Theorem 1.2.18.

In a circle of radius \(r\text{,}\) the arc length, \(s\text{,}\) subtended by a central angle (in radians), \(\theta\text{,}\) is

\begin{equation*} s=r\theta \end{equation*}

If \(\theta\) is given in degrees, then \(\displaystyle s=2\pi r\cdot\left(\frac{\theta}{360^{\circ}}\right)\text{.}\)
