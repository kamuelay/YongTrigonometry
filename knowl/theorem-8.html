Theorem 5.1.4. Conversion from Polar Coordinates to Rectangular Coordinates.

Given a point \(P\) with rectangular coordinates \((x,y)\text{,}\) the polar coordinates \((r,\theta)\) of \(P\) are found by

\begin{equation*} r^2=x^2+y^2 \tan\theta=\frac{y}{x},\mbox{~if~} x\neq0 \end{equation*}
