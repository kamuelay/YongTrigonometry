Solution 1.2.10.1.

  1. Since \(90^{\circ}\lt 140^{\circ}\lt 180^{\circ}\text{,}\) \(140^{\circ}\) lies in Quadrant II, or Manu Hoʻolua.

  2. Since \(-770^{\circ}\lt 0\text{,}\) we first add \(3\times360\) to \(-770^{\circ}\) to obtain an angle \(0^{\circ}\) and \(360^{\circ}\text{,}\)

    \begin{equation*} -770^{\circ}+3\times360^{\circ}=310^{\circ} \end{equation*}

    So \(310^{\circ}\) and \(-770^{\circ}\) are coterminal. Since \(270^{\circ}\lt 310^{\circ}\lt 360^{\circ}\text{,}\) \(310^{\circ}\) lies in Quadrant IV, or Manu Malanai.

  3. Since \(923^{\circ}>360\) we begin by subtracting \(2\times360^{\circ}\)

    \begin{equation*} 923^{\circ}-2\times360^{\circ}=203^{\circ} \end{equation*}

    So \(203^{\circ}\) and \(923^{\circ }\) are coterminal. Since \(180^{\circ}\lt 203^{\circ}\lt 270^{\circ}\text{,}\) \(923^{\circ}\) lies in Quadrant III or ʻĀina Kona.
