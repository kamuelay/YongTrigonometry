We will first convert \(24'\) and \(45''\) to degrees.

\begin{equation*} 24'=24\cdot1'=24\cdot\left(\frac{1}{60}\right)^{\circ}=0.4^{\circ} \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} 45''=45\cdot1''=45\cdot\left(\frac{1}{3600}\right)^{\circ}=0.0125^{\circ} \end{equation*}

So \(263^{\circ}24'45''=263^{\circ}+24'+45''=263^{\circ}+0.4^{\circ}+0.0125=263.4125^{\circ}\)
