Solution 3.2.1.1.

First note that \(105^{\circ}=60^{\circ}+45^{\circ}\text{.}\) Now, using the Sum Formula for Cosine,

\begin{align*} \cos105^{\circ}\amp =\amp \cos(60^{\circ}+45^{\circ})\\ \amp =\amp \cos60^{\circ}\cos45^{\circ}-\sin60^{\circ}\sin45^{\circ}\\ \amp =\amp \frac{1}{2}\cdot\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}-\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\\ \amp =\amp \frac{1}{4}\left(\sqrt{2}-\sqrt{6}\right) \end{align*}
