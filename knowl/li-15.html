Item 1.

We are given the angular speed is \(\omega=25\) revolutions per minute. To convert our angular speed to radians per minute, we use the fact that one revolution is \(2\pi\) radians to get

\begin{equation*} \omega=25\frac{\mbox{revolution} }{\mbox{minute} }=25\frac{\mbox{rev} }{\mbox{minute} }\cdot2\pi\frac{\mbox{radians} }{\mbox{revolution} }=50\pi\frac{\mbox{radians} }{\mbox{minute} } \end{equation*}

Thus the hoe uli is moving at an angular speed of \(50\pi\) radians per second.
