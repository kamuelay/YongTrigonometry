We can derive another formula for \(\tan\frac{\theta}{2}\) that does not involve \(\pm\text{:}\) \begin{mybox}[Half-Angle Formulas for Tangent]

\begin{equation*} \tan\frac{\theta}{2}=\pm\sqrt{\frac{1-\cos\theta}{1+\cos\theta}}=\frac{1-\cos\theta}{\sin\theta}=\frac{\sin\theta}{1+\cos\theta} \end{equation*}
