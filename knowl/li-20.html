Item 1.

When \(\theta=0\) radians (\(0^{\circ}\)), the point on the circle is \(P(1,0)\text{.}\)

Then \(x=1\) and \(y=0\) gives us

\begin{align*} \sin 0=\sin 0^{\circ}\amp =0, \amp \cos 0=\cos 0^{\circ}\amp =1, \amp \tan 0=\tan 0^{\circ}\amp =0,\\ \csc 0=\csc 0^{\circ}\amp =\mbox{undefined} , \amp \sec 0=\sec 0^{\circ}\amp =1, \amp \cot 0=\cot 0^{\circ}\amp =\mbox{undefined} \text{.} \end{align*}
