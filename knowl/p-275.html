\Question A waʻa sails in the direction of the house Nālani Hoʻolua for one day (120 nautical miles). How many nautical miles has the waʻa traveled north? How many miles has the waʻa traveled west? To calculate the angle \(\theta\text{,}\) refer to the Star Compass on Figure 1.1.1 on page 1.1.1 for help determining the number of houses and use the fact that one house is \(11.25^{\circ}\text{.}\)
