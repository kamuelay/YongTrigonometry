Solution 3.2.2.1.

Using the Difference Formula for Cosine we get

\begin{align*} \cos\left(\frac{\pi}{4}-\frac{\pi}{6}\right)\amp =\amp \cos\frac{\pi}{4}\cos\frac{\pi}{6}+\sin\frac{\pi}{4}\sin\frac{\pi}{6}\\ \amp =\amp \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\cdot\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}+\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\cdot\frac{1}{2}\\ \amp =\amp \frac{1}{4}\left(\sqrt{6}+\sqrt{2}\right) \end{align*}
