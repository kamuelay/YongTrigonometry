Recall that if \(\theta\) is an angle with radian measure \(t\text{,}\) then the six trigonometric functions become

\begin{align*} \sin \theta\amp =y \amp \cos \theta\amp =x \amp \tan \theta\amp =\frac{y}{x} (x\neq0)\\ \csc \theta\amp =\frac{1}{y} (y\neq0) \amp \sec \theta\amp =\frac{1}{x} (x\neq0) \amp \cot \theta\amp =\frac{x}{y} (y\neq0) \end{align*}
