Item 1.

  1. In Quadrant II, the associated angle is \(t=\pi-\frac{\pi}{3}=\frac{3\pi}{3}-\frac{\pi}{3}=\frac{2\pi}{3}\) Alternative solution: Since \(t'=\frac{\pi}{3}=\frac{1\pi}{3}\text{,}\) then \(t=\frac{(3-1)\pi}{3}=\frac{2\pi}{3}\)

  2. In Quadrant III, the associated angle is \(t=\pi+\frac{\pi}{3}=\frac{3\pi}{3}+\frac{\pi}{3}=\frac{4\pi}{3}\) Alternative solution: \(t=\frac{(3+1)\pi}{3}=\frac{4\pi}{3}\)

  3. In Quadrant IV, the associated angle is \(t=2\pi-\frac{\pi}{3}=\frac{6\pi}{3}-\frac{\pi}{3}=\frac{5\pi}{3}\) Alternative solution: \(t=\frac{(2\cdot3-1)\pi}{3}=\frac{5\pi}{3}\)
