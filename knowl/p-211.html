You may recognize the symmetry of \(\sin\theta\) and \(\cos\theta\) if reverse order of the values of sine and cosine from \(90^{\circ}\) to \(0^{\circ}\text{.}\) This symmetry gives us \(\sin0^{\circ}=\cos90^{\circ}\text{,}\) \(\sin30^{\circ}=\cos60^{\circ}\text{,}\) \(\sin45^{\circ}=\cos45^{\circ}\text{,}\) \(\sin60^{\circ}=\cos30^{\circ}\text{,}\) and \(\sin90^{\circ}=\cos0^{\circ}\text{.}\)
