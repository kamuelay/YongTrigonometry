For example, if a star arrives in the Koʻolau (northeastern) quadrant in the star house Noio, it will arc overhead, staying in the northern hemisphere and enters the horizon in the same house it arrived in, Noio, but in the Hoʻolua (northwest) quadrant. If a star arrives in the Malanai (southeast) quadrant in the house Lā, it will remain in the southern hemisphere as it arcs overhead and enters the horizon in the house Lā in the Kona (southwestern) quadrant.
